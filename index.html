My Journey

Born: 2003 Combined Military Hospital (ARMY) in Ghatail, Tangail.
I was two and a half years old when I first shifted to Chittagong.
In 2009, I got admitted to Patenga Islamia Fazil (Degree) Madrasah for nursery and studied there till 2011.
Then in 2012, I went to Narayanganj for eight months and later returned to Chittagong.
While in Chittagong, I started Hefz and shifted to Khulna in 2018. I completed Hefz in 2019 and got admitted to class eight in 2020 in Gilatola Ahmadia Dakhil Madrasah.
Later, I taught there for nine and ten and took the Dakhil examination in 2023.
Later, I got admitted to Khulna Nesaria Kamil Madrasah for Alim and I will take the Alim examination this year in 2025.
Thanks for visiting my website
